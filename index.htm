Overview

Below are a few important information about the modern language courses for Spring 2024.
  1. Placement exam
    If students are already proficient in one of the modern languages that we offer, such as Spanish, they must take the placement exam to determine their level in that language. Students with no prior knowledge of the language may enroll directly in the 101-level course without the placement exam. The placement exam is administered at the Modern Language Lab (B-206). Please see https://lagccml.github.io/#placementExam or https://www.laguardia.edu/ela/#modern-langlab for more information about the placement exam.
